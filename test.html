Service Refund Policy

Effective:

At Bolean Buildsers, we are committed to delivering exceptional software development services including custom application development, web development, and related consulting services. This refund policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be granted for our professional services.

1. Service Engagement Terms

  • All service engagements begin with a signed agreement or statement of work (SOW) outlining project scope, deliverables, and payment terms.
  • Payments are typically structured in milestones with specific deliverables.
  • Refund eligibility depends on the project phase and work completed.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • Initial Deposit: Refundable within 7 days if no work has commenced.
  • Milestone Payments: Partially refundable if deliverables are not met, proportional to work completed.
  • Completed Projects: No refunds after final delivery and acceptance.

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following are strictly non-refundable:

  • Completed work that meets agreed specifications
  • Consulting hours already expended
  • Third-party costs (hosting, APIs, licenses, etc.)
  • Projects where requirements change mid-development
  • Services terminated by client after 50% completion

4. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Submit a written request to support@boleanbuilders.com
  2. Include project reference number and detailed justification
  3. Our team will review within 10 business days
  4. Approved refunds processed within 15 business days

5. Dispute Resolution

  • We prefer to resolve issues through additional development rather than refunds
  • Unresolved disputes may be submitted to mediation
  • All claims must be made within 30 days of project completion
